Welcome

Welcome to NARBE House web apps and tools

This is the home for our accessible tools and editors that support Ben and families like ours. Game editors and builders for titles like Ben's P3GL Adventure and Ben's Mini Golf will live here as they are released.

Start here

For now, please try Benny's Accessibility Hub Beta. It includes a predictive keyboard, a customizable phrase and media board, and early games that work with two switches mapped to Space and Return. It also supports mouse or head and eye tracking where available.

Open Benny's Hub Beta Phrase Board Editor

Links: /bennyshub and /bennyshub/phraseboard/phrase-builder.html

Coming soon

  • Level editors for P3GL Adventure and Mini Golf
  • Import and export of levels and boards
  • Simple sharing flow for families and caregivers
  • Docs and quick start guides

We build with simplicity, clarity, and accessibility in mind.
